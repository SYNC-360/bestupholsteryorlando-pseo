```xml

<urlset xmlns="http://www.sitemaps.org/schemas/sitemap/0.9">
  <url>
    <loc>https://[domain]/[page-url]</loc>
    <lastmod>[YYYY-MM-DD]</lastmod>
    <changefreq>[daily/weekly/monthly]</changefreq>
    <priority>[0.5-1.0]</priority>
  </url>
</urlset>

<!-- Priority levels:
- Homepage: 1.0
- Main service pages: 0.9
- Location pages: 0.8  
- Blog/content: 0.7
- Supporting pages: 0.5
-->
```
